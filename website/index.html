Born in São Paulo city in 1996. I have been a technology enthusiast since I was seven. I've been pushing myself forward to learn new technologies since that time. I love learning new skills and solving problems, especially when these challenges can teach me something new.

Being self-taught, I see this path as an exciting journey with no end. Seeing the world through this mentality motivates me to move forward, learning everything that I can, such as new tools, technologies, and languages.

My Journey

I started my journey in computer maintenance during my childhood. I would fix computers or anything that I thought I could possibly fix with the knowledge I had back in the day.

After turning twelve, I started to learn video editing and image editing; this helped me become a designer some years later. As a designer, my main task was to help people create their own visual identity, especially creators who worked with digital content.

In my twenties, I started working on computer maintenance while still doing some design work. Additionally, I started learning front-end development, which shifted my focus to programming. Since then, I've been learning everything I can to expand my knowledge and build a career as a developer.
